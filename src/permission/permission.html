Microphone access

Recaptain needs permission to record your microphone. Click below — Chrome will show a prompt in the address bar.

Doesn't work? Mic might already be blocked for this extension.
  1. Open chrome://settings/content/microphone.
  2. Scroll to Not allowed to use your microphone.
  3. Remove any entry for chrome-extension://.
  4. Come back here and click Grant again.
